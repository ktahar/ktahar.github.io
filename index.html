This is personal website of Kosuke Tahara (田原 康佐).

About

Kosuke Tahara is a researcher at Toyota Central R&D Labs., Inc. He received Doctor of Engineering from Tokyo Institute of Technology in 2016. He had worked on researches of electronic materials and devices there. His current research interests include robotics, machine learning, and mathematical optimization.
